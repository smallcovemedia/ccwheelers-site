Zones

  • Sand Highway: street-legal beach corridor from Grand Ave to Post 2. Highway 1 rules apply to Pole 11.
  • OHV Riding Area: open riding from Post 2 south to the boundary fence. Green sticker required.
  • Beach & Dune Camping: primitive camping south of Post 2. Reserve at (800) 444-7275.
  • Closed / Sensitive: no vehicles. Nesting exclosures for the snowy plover and least tern (Mar 1 to Sep 30).
